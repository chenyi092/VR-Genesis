影展簡介

VR Genesis 是全球最具代表性的國際VR盛會，將在2025年於台灣台中舉行。本屆的策展主題「VRelationship」是由VR與Relationship這兩個單字組合而成，透過虛擬實境的形式去探索人與人之間的單純又複雜關係。情感，是維繫關係的橋樑，真摯的親情，純真的友情，深刻的愛情，是發生在你我之間最真實的感受。因此，今年的VR Genesis不只是一場VR博覽會，更是一場人際關係的探索旅程，期待透過虛擬實境的藝術創作：電影、裝置、劇場、遊戲類型的作品進行一場跨越國際的情感交流，讓不同國籍、不同年齡層的觀眾都能享受這場以關係為主題的VR盛會。

 

 

VR Genesis is the most representative international VR global eventand will be held in Taichung, Taiwan in 2025. This year's curatorial theme "VRelationship" is a combination of VR and Relationship., aiming to explore the simple and complex relationships between people through the form of virtual reality. Emotion is the bridge that maintains relationships. Therefore, this year's VR Genesis is not just a VR expo but also an exploration journey of interpersonal relationships. We look forward to a cross-international emotional journey through virtual reality : movies, installations, theaters, and games works, allowing audiences from different nationalities and ages to enjoy this relationship-themed VR event.
